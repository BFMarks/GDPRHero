The Problem:

App marketers use numerous third-party solutions to acquire, engage and retain their users but have no streamlined way to manage GDPR compliance across these data partners.

  • Possible millions of dollars in fines.
  • Risk of passing Personal Identifiable Information to untrustworthy third party vendors.
  • Too many integrations to manage.

The GDPR Hero Team is here to save the day!

GDPR Hero was built by mobile sass insiders who identified the common need for automated GDPR Compliance for app owners who rely on multiple third-party solutions to acquire, engage and retain their users, but have no easy way to manage the cumbersome requirements of GDPR.

  • Easily turn on (or off) PII information from being passed back to vendors.
  • Ensure your system is completely free from legal blowback.
  • Speak with a Silicon Valley experts.
